# Heat Pump Cost Calculator - Content Strategy

## 1. Core Value Proposition
**"The most accurate, up-to-date estimator for heat pump installation costs and savings."**
Targeting homeowners who are considering switching from gas/oil to electric heat pumps.

## 2. Content Pillars

### A. Cost & Budgeting (Transactional)
*Focus: Helping users estimate immediate costs.*
- **Primary Keywords**: `heat pump cost calculator`, `installation cost`, `cost per sq ft`.
- **Content Type**: Tool Pages, Cost Guides.

### B. Savings & Efficiency (Commercial/Informational)
*Focus: ROI and long-term value.*
- **Primary Keywords**: `heat pump savings`, `energy efficiency`, `tax credits 2026`.
- **Content Type**: Calculators, Comparison Articles.

### C. Technology & Types (Informational)
*Focus: Educating on different systems.*
- **Primary Keywords**: `ductless vs ducted`, `air source vs geothermal`, `cold climate heat pumps`.
- **Content Type**: Deep-dive Guides, FAQs.

## 3. Site Architecture & URL Structure

### Tools (The Magnets)
- `/` (Home/Calculator) - Main "Heat Pump Cost Calculator".
- `/tools/savings-estimator` - "Heat Pump Savings Calculator" (ROI focus).

### Guides (The Authority Builders)
- `/guides/heat-pump-cost-2026` - Comprehensive cost breakdown.
- `/guides/heat-pump-vs-gas-furnace` - Comparative analysis.
- `/guides/ductless-mini-split-cost` - Specific system type guide.
- `/guides/geothermal-heat-pump-cost` - Specific system type guide.

### FAQ (The Snippet Winners)
- `/faq` - Structured data-rich FAQ page.
  - "How much does a heat pump cost for a 2000 sq ft house?"
  - "Are heat pumps worth it in cold climates?"

## 4. Internal Linking Strategy
- **Calculator** link to **Cost Guide** for detailed breakdown.
- **Cost Guide** link to **Calculator** for personalized estimate.
- **Comparison Articles** link to **Savings Estimator**.

## 5. Success Metrics
- **Rankings**: Top 3 for "heat pump cost calculator".
- **Engagement**: Time on page > 2 mins (Calculator usage).
- **Conversion**: Request for quote (future feature).
